Who I Am

A Little About Me

For the past 7 years, I've been building enterprise-scale CRM solutions on the Salesforce platform. From automating guided agent workflows with OmniStudio at Delta Air Lines to migrating legacy automation and building full-cycle hiring portals at Bigworks Technologies. I write Apex, design Lightning Web Components, architect complex Flows, and connect enterprise systems through REST, SOAP, and MuleSoft integrations. That experience taught me how to turn messy business requirements into clean, scalable code that operations teams actually rely on.

I'm currently pursuing a Master's in Artificial Intelligence. Salesforce is evolving into an intelligence layer, and I want to be the developer who bridges deep platform expertise with AI capabilities, whether that's Einstein Analytics, predictive automation, or intelligent agent workflows. My goal is to build solutions that are both technically solid and genuinely smart.

I hold a Salesforce Certified Platform Developer I (PD1) certification and a Master of Computer Applications from JNTU Kakinada. Based in Austin, TX. Always happy to talk enterprise architecture, intelligent automation, or where CRM is heading next.
